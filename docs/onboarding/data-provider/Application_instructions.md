To join the Traffic Flow Data Space (TFDS), you must adhere to the governance and legal frameworks. This overviews your rights and responsibilities.

As a Data Provider and Data Holder, you retain sovereign control over your information. You maintain the right to give and retract consent for its use. You define usage policies, licensing terms, and conditions for your offerings. Ownership remains entirely with you or the original licensor, and your work will be visibly credited. Every exchange is secured through formal digital contract negotiations managed by the SIMPL-open connector.

Your operational duty involves managing the lifecycle and quality of your data assets. You must warrant that all processing and sharing activities comply with European and national legislation, including the GDPR, Data Governance Act, and Open Data Directive. Technical interoperability specifications are detailed within the application.

To formalize participation, sign and submit the attached TFDS Membership Agreement. This document binds you to the rules, details the dispute resolution mechanism, and outlines terms for voluntary or involuntary off-boarding. Ensure Appendix A is completed with the TFDS Technical Authority to establish your onboarding schedule.
