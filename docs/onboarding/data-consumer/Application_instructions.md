To join the Traffic Flow Data Space (TFDS), you must adhere to the governance and legal frameworks. This overviews your rights and responsibilities.

As a Data Consumer, you are granted access to data within the federated environment, provided you utilize it strictly in accordance with the licenses and usage policies defined by the Data Providers. All data access must respect the sovereign control of the data holders and adhere to the machine-readable policies attached to each catalogue offering. Every data exchange you engage in requires a formal digital contract negotiation managed by the SIMPL-open connector.

You must warrant that all your data processing and usage activities comply with applicable European and national legislation. This includes strict adherence to the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) for any protected data you process, the Data Governance Act, and the Open Data Directive. Technical interoperability specifications are detailed within the application.

To formalize participation, sign and submit the attached TFDS Membership Agreement. This document binds you to the rules, details the dispute resolution mechanism, and outlines terms for voluntary or involuntary off-boarding. Ensure Appendix A is completed with the TFDS Technical Authority to establish your onboarding schedule.
